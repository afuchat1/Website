# AfuChat Technologies Limited --- Master Website Prompt

> Build a production-ready, enterprise-grade corporate website for
> **AfuChat Technologies Limited**.

## Mission

Create a premium multi-page website that presents AfuChat as a connected
digital ecosystem, not just a messaging app.

## Company

-   Company: AfuChat Technologies Limited
-   Primary Product: AfuChat
-   Identity Platform: AfuMail
-   Vision: One connected account for every AfuChat service.

## Core Identity

Users create **one AfuMail account**. That account becomes the identity
provider for: - AfuChat - AfuAI - AfuCloud - AfuMovies - AfuMall -
AfuNews - AfuBlog - Developer Platform - Future products

Never require separate accounts.

## Design Requirements

-   No plain backgrounds anywhere.
-   Every section uses layered mesh gradients, SVG illustrations, glow
    effects, subtle patterns and tasteful animations.
-   Premium quality comparable to leading global technology companies
    without copying them.

## Performance

-   Responsive images
-   Fast rendering

## Important

Do **not** invent CEO, office address, legal identifiers or other
company facts. Use clearly marked placeholders where information has not
been supplied.

The final website should feel like the official online presence of a
global technology company while clearly communicating that AfuMail is
the foundation of the AfuChat ecosystem.
