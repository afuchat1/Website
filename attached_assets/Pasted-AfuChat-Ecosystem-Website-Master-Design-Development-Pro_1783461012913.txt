AfuChat Ecosystem Website – Master Design & Development Prompt

You are designing and building the official public website for AfuChat.

This is not just another messaging app landing page.

This website represents an entire digital ecosystem similar in ambition to the ecosystems built by companies like Google, Apple, Microsoft, Cloudflare, Stripe, and Notion. However, do not copy their designs. Create a unique visual identity for AfuChat while achieving the same level of polish, consistency, clarity, and quality.

---

First, understand the product

Before generating any UI, understand these facts.

AfuChat is an ecosystem.

It consists of multiple connected products that work together.

These include:

- AfuChat (Social platform & messaging)
- AfuMail (Email & digital identity)
- AfuAI (Artificial Intelligence)
- AfuCloud (Cloud storage)
- AfuMovies (Movie discovery)
- AfuMall (Commerce)
- AfuNews (News platform)
- AfuBlog (Publishing)
- Developer Platform (APIs, authentication, SDKs)
- Future products that can easily join the ecosystem

Never present these as unrelated apps.

Everything belongs to one ecosystem.

---

The most important concept

AfuMail is NOT just email.

AfuMail is the identity provider for the entire ecosystem.

Think of it as the authentication foundation.

Every user creates one AfuMail account.

That single account becomes the identity used everywhere.

There are no separate accounts for different products.

Flow:

User

↓

Create AfuMail

Access granted automatically to:

• AfuChat

• Every future AfuChat product

The website must make visitors understand this visually before they even read the text.

Final Objective

The finished website should make visitors instantly understand three things:

1. AfuChat is a complete digital ecosystem, not just a messaging app.

2. AfuMail is the foundation of the ecosystem. Users create one AfuMail account, and that single identity securely unlocks every current and future AfuChat service without creating additional accounts.

3. AfuChat is building a connected digital future where communication, AI, email, cloud storage, shopping, movies, news, publishing, and developer tools all work together through one unified identity.

Every design decision, illustration, animation, interaction, background, and layout should reinforce these three ideas. The website should feel like it belongs to a global technology company while remaining uniquely AfuChat.
